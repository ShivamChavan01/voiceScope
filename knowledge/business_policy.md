# VoiceScope Business Policy

## Refund Policy

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days from the date of approval. Refund requests must be submitted within 30 days of the original purchase date. Refunds are issued to the original payment method only. Partial refunds may be issued for items returned in damaged condition.

Refunds exceeding $50 require supervisor approval before processing. Agents cannot guarantee same-day refunds under any circumstances. Refund processing times may be extended during holiday periods.

## Exchange Policy

Exchanges are accepted within 30 days of purchase. Items must be returned in original packaging and in resalable condition. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the exchange is due to a defective product or shipping error. Exchanges for items of lesser value will be refunded the difference to the original payment method.

## Escalation Rules

Issues unresolved after 2 calls must be escalated to a supervisor. If a customer requests to speak with a supervisor, the request must be honored immediately without requiring a reason. Billing disputes exceeding $100 must be escalated to the billing department. Any threat of legal action must be immediately escalated to the legal team and the call must be documented.

## Agent Restrictions

Agents cannot guarantee same-day refunds. Agents cannot issue refunds exceeding $50 without supervisor approval. Agents cannot promise inventory availability without verifying current stock levels. Agents cannot offer discounts or promotions not listed in the current approved offers system. Agents cannot access or modify customer payment information directly. Agents must not make promises about delivery times that are not confirmed by the shipping system.

## Shipping Policy

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. Free shipping is available on orders over $50. International shipping times vary by destination and are not guaranteed. Lost shipments must be reported within 14 days of the expected delivery date.

## Data Privacy

Agents must not share customer personal information with unauthorized parties. Customer data requests must be directed to the privacy team. Call recordings are retained for 90 days unless the customer requests deletion. Agents must verify customer identity before discussing account details.
